  • May 6, 2026: PACT 2026 Tutorials Are Now Available Online

    PACT 2026 is pleased to announce that the tutorial program is now available online. The tutorials will take place on Monday, October 19, 2026, and will offer attendees the opportunity to engage with timely topics, tools, and methods in parallel architectures, compilation techniques, and related areas. We encourage participants to review the tutorial offerings and plan their PACT 2026 attendance accordingly. Tutorials are included at no additional fee with either full conference registration or one-day registration for Monday, October 19. The full tutorial program is available here: https://pact2026.github.io/workshops

  • April 16, 2026: HPE Supports PACT 2026 as a Silver Sponsor

    PACT 2026 is pleased to recognize HPE as a Silver Sponsor of the conference. HPE’s support helps strengthen the conference and the broader PACT community. We are grateful for their participation and for their commitment to advancing research in parallel architectures and compilation techniques.

  • April 10, 2026: Student Support Available for PACT 2026

    Thanks to support from SIGARCH, limited student support is available for PACT 2026. If you are a student planning to attend, please contact the General Chair at mtaufer@utk.edu with PACT student support in the subject line. We encourage interested students to reach out early.
